3D Open World Prototype

A 3D open world prototype made with Unity.

This project was a bit too ambitious. I wanted to create a kind of open-world game where the players could walk, drive around, and interact with other pedestrians and shops.

Basic Idea

The 3D assets were all from the unity asset store. I made a prototype scene by arranging some of the buidlings.

Then I create a script that would randomly spawn different pedestrians from a predefined collection. I added another similar script but for the cars on the street.

The challenging part was to work on the car driving logic and the animations. One specific problem I was facing with portraying the correct amount of car wheel spin based on the amount of speed/acceleration of the vehicle.

Retrospective

Overall, the game is still unfinished and has many features that were a bit too ambitious for me for that time. I do plan to revisit it in the near future and work on adding/completing few more features/
